About

From Karakoram
to the Seine

A research path shaped by mountains, megacities, and a French river.

I am a PhD candidate at the Institut Polytechnique UniLaSalle Beauvais (Unité de recherche AGHYLE), working in collaboration with SIAAP on water quality prediction for the Seine River. My thesis focuses on qualifying multivariate online data, developing water quality indicators, and modelling with satellite imagery — with the applied goal of enabling real-time bacteriological risk assessment for Seine swimming.

Before France, I spent over a year as a Research Assistant at the Department of Land Surveying and Geo-Informatics (LSGI) at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, where I published on land cover simulation, groundwater dynamics, and SAR-based deformation detection. Prior to that, I worked nearly four years as a GIS Officer at the Aga Khan Agency for Habitat Pakistan, applying remote sensing and geospatial tools to disaster risk management in the Karakoram and Hindukush regions.

My research integrates remote sensing and geospatial technologies to address climate change impacts, hazard vulnerability, and water resource management, using machine learning to analyse large-scale earth observation datasets.

Research Areas

What I Work On

Six intersecting domains across environmental remote sensing, GIS, and AI.

🛰️

Satellite Water Quality

Probabilistic water pixel extraction (Monte Carlo / Sentinel-2), turbidity and suspended matter retrieval, and bacteriological proxy modelling for the Seine River.

🌍

Land Cover & Change Detection

ML-based classification in complex mountainous landscapes; land cover simulation and future scenario modelling for the Greater Bay Area of China.

📡

SAR & InSAR

Sentinel-SAR interferometry for land subsidence and deformation in megacities (Karachi); multi-temporal InSAR for creep-type landslide dynamics.

⚠️

Hazard & Disaster Risk

Debris flow analysis and risk mapping in Gilgit-Baltistan and Chitral; ecotourism and environmental assessment using GIS, AHP, and crowd-sourced data.

🌊

Groundwater & Hydrology

Hydrogeochemical and geospatial assessment of groundwater quality; detecting groundwater upsurge in coastal cities from satellite imagery.

🧠

AI for Earth Observation

Machine learning and deep learning for large-scale EO datasets; systematic review of AI-driven approaches for river water quality retrieval and WQI methods.
